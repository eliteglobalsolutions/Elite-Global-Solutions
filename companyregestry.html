Who This Service Is For

This service is designed for entrepreneurs, micro-business owners and international operators who require a clean, compliant structure to support cross-border clients, payments and growth.

Typical use cases:

  • Launching a new international consulting, e-commerce or digital business.
  • Relocating an existing structure from one jurisdiction to another.
  • Separating international activities from a domestic entity for clarity and risk management.
  • Adding holding or IP entities in reputable, flexible jurisdictions.

What you get:

  • Guidance on suitable jurisdictions based on your goals and risk profile.
  • Coordination with local lawyers, accountants and agents.
  • Support on document preparation, notarisation and apostille where needed.
  • Introductions to banking and payment partners where appropriate.
