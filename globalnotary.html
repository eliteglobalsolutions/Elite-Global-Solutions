GLOBAL NOTARY SERVICES
We provide notary services across 50+ countries, including Australia, the U.S., UK, Canada, Singapore, UAE, Hong Kong, and New Zealand.

Our services cover notarisation of personal and business documents, certified copies, affidavits, powers of attorney, and more. We assist with apostille and embassy legalisation through official authorities such as DFAT (Australia), SAL (Singapore), FCDO (UK), and state/federal agencies in the U.S.

Online and remote notarisation is available in select jurisdictions. Additional support includes document translation, certification, and secure international delivery.
