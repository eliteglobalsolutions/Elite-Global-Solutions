Who This Service Is For

Our tax advisory coordination is designed for individuals and businesses whose lives, clients or assets span more than one country.

We commonly assist:

  • Entrepreneurs operating companies in one country while living in another.
  • Professionals with global income streams (consulting, digital services, investments).
  • Clients considering a change of tax residency or relocation.
  • Owners of holding, IP or investment structures with cross-border exposure.

Our role:

  • Clarifying the questions you need to ask in each jurisdiction.
  • Connecting you with qualified tax advisors and accountants.
  • Coordinating between your legal, banking and tax teams.
  • Helping to translate complex advice into practical next steps.

We are not a law firm or tax practice. All tax advice is provided by licensed professionals in the relevant jurisdictions.

How We Work With You

Our aim is to reduce confusion and duplication by centralising coordination, while keeping specialist tax advice in the hands of licensed professionals.

1. Initial overview – You share your current structure (companies, trusts, key assets), countries involved and your short- to medium-term goals.

2. Mapping jurisdictions – We help you identify which countries are relevant for tax, banking and reporting and where specialist advice is required.

3. Professional referrals – We introduce you to tax advisors and accountants in the appropriate jurisdictions, based on your profile and language needs.

4. Coordination – As advice is provided, we assist with implementation steps such as company changes, notary, banking introductions and document preparation.

5. Ongoing support – We remain available to help coordinate updates as your situation evolves (new countries, entities or investments).

Nothing on this page is legal or tax advice. All decisions should be made with qualified professionals licensed in the relevant jurisdictions.
